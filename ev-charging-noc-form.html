Subject: Approval of NOC for EV Charging Point Installation

Dear Resident,

We acknowledge the receipt of your application requesting permission to install an Electric Vehicle (EV) charging point at your allocated parking space.

After reviewing the details submitted and verifying compliance with the safety and installation guidelines, TAAOWA hereby grants you the No Objection Certificate (NOC) to proceed with the installation of the EV charging point in your designated parking area.

Approval Conditions
  • Power will be drawn from your flat’s TNEB meter.
  • The charger will not be used for any commercial activity.
  • The installation will adhere strictly to the EV Charger SOP and safety standards specified by TAAOWA.
  • No structural modifications will be made without prior approval.
  • The charging unit will not obstruct movement or create safety hazards.
  • Any maintenance issues will be reported promptly to TAAOWA.
  • Resident takes full responsibility for the installed charger and its usage and any liabilities.
