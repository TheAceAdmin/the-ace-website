Available Positions

Principal Officers (5 Positions)

The post numbers from 1 to 5 called Principal Officers and directly elected by electoral voters.

1. President

Overall leadership and strategic direction of the association. Represents TAAOWA in all official matters.

2. Secretary

Maintain all official records, correspondence, and documentation. Ensure proper communication with members.

3. Vice President

Support the President and assume leadership in their absence. Focus on community engagement and member relations.

4. Joint Secretary

Support the Secretary in administrative tasks and focus on specific areas like maintenance and facilities.

5. Treasurer

Manage financial affairs, maintain accounts, and ensure transparent financial reporting to all members.

Board of Members (16 Positions)

16 board members has to be elected from all four blocks keeping the need of block wise representation too.

6. Board of Members - 16 Nos

Represent member interests, participate in decision-making, and contribute to various committees and initiatives.

Eligibility Criteria

Basic Eligibility Requirements

  • The contestant shall be a flat owner and also member of the Association
  • The contestant must be at least 18 years old
  • Only the owners who are staying as residents within the 'The Ace apartment' are eligible to contest the election at the time of nomination
  • Only the Primary Nominee provided in the Membership Form is eligible for candidature and voting
  • There can be a maximum of only one candidate from one family (even if the family owns multiple flats)
  • A candidate can contest for only one position. He/she cannot submit multiple nomination forms (to contest for different posts)
  • A member who wants to contest for Board of Members, he should apply for the post of the block in which his flat is present

Conflict of Interest Rules

If a member, or anyone belonging to a member's family, has any ongoing regular financial transactions with the Association, such member is not eligible to contest for any post of the Executive Committee for the duration of such financial transactions.

  • Lease/rental of facilities within the premises, where the lease/rent is payable to the Association, including the supermarket, grocery shop, vegetable shop, play school, hair salon, beauty parlour, doctor's clinic, juice shop, ironing shop, tailoring shop, etc.
  • Acting as a commercial service provider / vendor to the Association, such as facility management services, security services, housekeeping services, supply of water in tankers, maintenance contracts for generators, lifts, STP, fire hydrant, reticulated gas supply, gym, swimming pool, gardening, etc.
  • Any other contract works for the Association such as civil works, plumbing, electrical works, etc.

Election Process

Nomination Process

  • The Nomination form will be available online and can be submitted online too
  • The practice of nomination deposit is dispensed in this election
  • If only one nomination is received for a position, then that member will be declared elected unopposed for that position
  • For those positions where more than the number of posts nomination is received, the elections will be held through online electronic secret ballot method
  • Any candidate is permitted to contest only for one post in the total 21 posts election being held

Voting Process

  • Election will be conducted by the Election Conduction Officer as Returning Officer, preferably member or a resident of The Ace
  • Voting will be conducted through online electronic secret ballot method via MyGate
  • All the members of The Ace are entitled to cast vote for maximum up to four members of each block for Board Members
  • They can cast vote from 0,1,2,3 or 4 for each block
  • One flat, one member in association, one vote

Result Declaration

  • Counting and declaration of result will be done with presence of 3 owners-member of association, Returning officer, and Advocate of association
  • If any Principal Officer post remains vacant the new elected committee may coopt a member from the member of association from the voter list
  • If any vacancy remains after election new Board of manager committee may coopt any member from the eligible voter used during the election

Mock Polls

As it is proposed to use electronic voting method 'mock polls' will be demonstrated at The Ace premises at certain interval of days well before the election, which will be notified. The candidate who are in fray/planning to contest can see the process and test the methods used.
